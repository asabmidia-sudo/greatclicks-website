Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Greatclicks.io ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Greatclicks.io website and provides done-for-you GoHighLevel (GHL) automation services for functional and integrative medicine practitioners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, business name, and other contact details you provide when signing up for our services, submitting a form, or contacting us.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through our payment processor, Whop. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, device information, and referring URLs.
  • GoHighLevel Account Data: Information related to your GHL sub-account that we access in order to build and manage automations on your behalf.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our done-for-you automation services
  • Process transactions and manage your subscription
  • Communicate with you about your account, service updates, and support requests
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Analyze usage trends and website performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics data. Specifically, we use:

  • Vercel Analytics: Our website is hosted on Vercel, and we use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymized performance and usage data.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site, including page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Google Analytics may set cookies on your browser.
  • Essential Cookies: Cookies necessary for the basic functionality of our website.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party services to operate our business. Each has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information:

  • GoHighLevel (GHL): The CRM and automation platform we build and manage on your behalf.
  • Whop: Our payment processor for subscription billing and checkout.
  • Vercel: Our website hosting provider and analytics platform.
  • Google Analytics: Website traffic and usage analytics.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information with these service providers as necessary to deliver our services.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use strong passwords and safeguard your account credentials.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@greatclicks.io.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us at hello@greatclicks.io and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Continued use of our website and services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
