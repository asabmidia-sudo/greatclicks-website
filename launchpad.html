What is inside The Launchpad

A complete client conversion system, wired together and live inside your GHL account.

01

Website Form

A custom intake or inquiry form embedded on your website, connected directly to your GHL pipeline so every submission lands in the right place automatically.

02

Discovery Call Funnel

A booking page built with FDN-specific copy using root-cause health messaging and the "tired of band-aid solutions" angle. Designed to attract the right patients and convert browsers into booked calls.

03

Appointment Automation

Automated SMS and email reminders for every discovery call, sent at the right intervals so your no-show rate drops before your subscription even begins.

04

No-Show Recovery

An automated missed call text-back and follow-up sequence so no lead slips through when you are with a patient or away from the phone.

05

Pipeline Setup

A clean, simple pipeline view inside GHL: New Lead, Nurturing, Discovery Booked, Discovery Completed, Client Won, Client Lost. You always know exactly where every contact stands.

06

Lead Magnet Funnel

An opt-in page for a guide like "5 Hidden Stressors Keeping You Sick" or "What Your Doctor Isn't Testing," built to capture cold leads and move them into your nurture system automatically.

07

Contact Tagging

Systematic tagging applied across your pipeline so every contact is categorized by source, status, and interest, making your list clean and actionable from day one.

08

14-Day Email Nurture Sequence

Fourteen days of emails that educate your leads on functional lab work, explain why FDN is different from conventional medicine, and build enough trust that booking a discovery call feels like the obvious next step.

09

Email Newsletter Templates

A set of branded, ready-to-use newsletter templates inside GHL so sending ongoing communication to your list never requires starting from scratch.

10

Programs and Packages Setup

Your core offers configured inside GHL with functional checkout pages, so when a discovery call converts, the next step is seamless for both you and your new client.

11

Post-Purchase Automation

A welcome email sequence and program access workflow triggered the moment someone buys, so every new client gets a consistent, professional onboarding experience without you lifting a finger.

12

SMS Feature

Available on request. If you want SMS added to any part of the system, from nurture to reminders to post-purchase onboarding, we wire it in as part of The Launchpad.
